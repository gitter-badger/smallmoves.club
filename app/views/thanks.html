Welcome Aboard the Small Moves Club!

Was that a 'small' signup form, or what? Keep an eye out for an invite email any minute now to the Slack chat community and activate your account there, so you can meet your new fellow Small Movers.

So what's next? Once you've joined the Slack, introduce yourself to the others in the #general channel or join in on the #dailystandup action by saying three things: What did you get done last, what are you working on next and what are you stuck on? Add an optional mood emoji and repeat that as often as you can and then show off something you've completed in the #shipped channel. If it's not quite ready yet, ask for comments on #feedback or get help on #advice if you feel like you're stuck in a rut.

Share Small Moves Club on Twitter or follow us back to see when community members celebrate their little victories.

Problems or questions? Send an email!
