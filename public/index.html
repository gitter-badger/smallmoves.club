Small Moves Club

A motivational community to celebrate little victories.

  • Tell Me More

A place for makers to cheer each other on as they finally go from thinking about that thing to actually doing it.

Lots of us have ideas. And then we realize things always seem just a little more complicated than we thought. But they don’t have to be.
We’re a Slack chat centric community (for now) with one humble goal: Help people to make their tiny dreams become a reality, and then build on top of that for something bigger.

Share your progress

The Daily Standup channel asks people to say what they did yesterday, what they plan to do today and what they’re currently stuck on. Go on the record with your commitment.

Get advice and feedback

Find inspiration by discussing your ideas and get concrete tips and opinions on what you’ve accomplished so far. Get a virtual pat on the back when you’ve shipped something.

Find an accountability buddy

Team up with someone you share similar goals with and nudge each other towards them. They’ve got your permission to be all over you about your progress.

Ready to become a Small Mover?

Small Moves Club isn’t just for tech products. You don’t even have to ‘ship’ something for the public. Write a short story, work on your garden, learn a new language, start a new hobby. Even if learning to code or launching an app isn’t your goal, we hope our method can give you that gentle push you needed to accomplish something you can be proud of.
